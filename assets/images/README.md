# Kenzy Kreates — Photos

## The photos in `stock/` are TEMPORARY placeholders

They are free-license Pexels photos (see `stock/CREDITS.md`) standing in until
Adam & McKenzie's real photos are ready. **Real photos of your actual bakes will
always sell better than stock** — locals can tell.

## How to swap in your real photos

1. Take your photo (tips below) and save it as a `.jpg`.
2. Rename it to EXACTLY the filename it replaces (see the list below).
3. Drop it into this `stock/` folder, replacing the old file.
4. Commit + push to GitHub (the site auto-deploys), then purge LiteSpeed Cache
   in WP Admin (the lightning-bolt icon, top bar → Purge All).

That's it — no code changes needed. Optional: also add a `.webp` version with
the same name (e.g. `brownies.webp`) and the site serves it automatically to
modern browsers for faster loading.

## Photo tips (what makes bakery photos sell)

- **Natural light**: shoot beside a window, light coming from the side,
  2–3 feet away. Never use the overhead kitchen light or flash.
- **Consistent backdrops**: pick 2–3 surfaces (a wood board, a neutral cloth,
  your counter) and reuse them for everything — consistency reads professional.
- **Don't over-edit**: bump brightness a touch if needed; heavy saturation is
  the #1 giveaway of amateur photos.
- **Show the good stuff**: a cookie broken in half, a brownie stack with a
  fudgy middle, the cross-section of a filled treat.
- A recent phone camera is completely fine. No fancy camera needed.
